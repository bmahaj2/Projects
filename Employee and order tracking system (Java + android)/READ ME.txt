---------READ ME-------------
1) Install android adt setup on your computer.
2) Install NETBEANS setup on your computer.
3) Install MySQL setup on your computer.
4)Import Employee Trackers project into the workspace.
5)Import employeeadmin project into the workspace.
6)Import GPS project into the NETBEANS.
7)Import database tables into the MY SQL WORKBENCH.
8)Install  USB driver for your phone on your computer.This driver is easily avaliable on official website of company.
9)Connect android phone to your computer with data cable.
10)In eclipse ,open run configuration -select project.
11) Select target as =active devices .
12)Now run the project,this will push the app onto your phone.
13)Now the app is successfully installed on your android phone.
14)remove data cable,run app.
